Mouse Move
INDEX FINGER UP
Raise only your index finger and move your hand to control the cursor on screen.
🤙
Left Click
MIDDLE FINGER ONLY
Keep index finger down, raise middle finger to trigger a left mouse click.
✌️
Right Click
INDEX UP + MIDDLE CURLED
Raise index finger while curling middle finger down to right click.
🤞
Double Click
INDEX + MIDDLE CLOSE
Bring index and middle fingertips close together to double click.
✊
Drag & Drop
FIST + MOVE
Make a fist and move your hand to drag, release fist to drop.
🖖
Scroll
INDEX + MIDDLE UP/DOWN
Raise index and middle fingers, move up or down to scroll the page.
🤏
Volume Control
PINCH VERTICAL
Pinch thumb and index together, move up to increase or down to decrease volume.
💡
Brightness
PINCH HORIZONTAL
Pinch thumb and index, move right to increase or left to decrease brightness.
